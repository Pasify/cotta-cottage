About Cotta Cottage

Our story, values, and the team who craft unforgettable stays

Our Mission

At Cotta Cottage we design and host handcrafted A-frame cabins that reconnect guests with nature while offering the comfort of modern design. We prioritise sustainability, local partnerships, and high-touch hospitality.

Our Story

Founded by a group of cabin-lovers and designers in 2021, Cotta Cottage grew from a single renovated A-frame into a collection of thoughtfully restored cabins across scenic locations. Each property is curated with local craft and materials.
